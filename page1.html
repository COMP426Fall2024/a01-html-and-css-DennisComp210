Main Characters From Journey to the West

  • Sun WuKong, also known as the Monkey King, was born from a stone and acquired supernatural powers and abilities through Taoist practices. After gaining his powers, he rebelled against the heaven but was defeated and imprisoned under a mountain for five hundred years.

  • Tang Sanzang, also known as Xuanzang, was a monk during the Tang dynasty that rescued Wukong from under the mountain. Accompanied by Wukong and his two other disciples, Zhu Bajie and Sha Wujing, Xuanzang journey to the west to retrieve the sacred Buddhist scriptures after enduring and overcoming 81 different challenges.

  • Zhu Bajie, was once a general for the heaven in human form, but got transformed into a pig-like creature after being banished from the heaven for his misconduct. He became Xuanzang's second disciple and protects Xuanzang on his journey to the west.

  • Sha Wujing, like Bajie, was once a general for the heaven. Also like Bajie, was banished from the heaven into the mortal world due to his misconduct. He became Xuanzang's third disciple and is mostly responsible for carring supplies and essentials.
